About Me

⌘

I'm Matt Ayre a web developer from Birmingham, UK. I have for over 2 years been developing for a range of clients; developing small business websites, creative ideas and web pages for friends, to blogs and github projects. Not only do I create websites and platforms, I also offer support, hosting/domain advice, and more.

With a genuiue interest and passion for design and web development, I am always learning new ways to improve what I can offer

I think you’ll be happy working with me on your project

I have made all my websites fully reponsive and optimised individually for use on all formats ranging from phone to large computers. All my web design work has up to now been intially built around the bootstrap web framework or related UI kits
